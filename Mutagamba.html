I'm Jasmine Martha Mutagamba, a Bachelor of Science in Data Science student at Uganda Christian University, Mukono. I'm passionate about using data as a tool for understanding — and ultimately improving — the world around me.

03 — Projects

Work I'm proud of

★ Featured project — Data Science
Graduate Unemployment Among University Graduates in Uganda

Investigated the growing crisis of graduate unemployment in Uganda — a country where a university degree no longer guarantees employment. This research project involved framing the core problem with precision, identifying structural gaps between academic training and labour market demands, and building a foundation for evidence-based policy recommendations.

The work required condensing a complex socioeconomic phenomenon into a tight, well-evidenced problem statement — a skill that sits at the heart of good data science: knowing exactly what question you are asking before you try to answer it.
